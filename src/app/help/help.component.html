Help - Features

Booking a Seat

To book a seat,click on the My Booking link from the side menu. Then, click on the Book a Seat button to select the seat details and confirm your booking.

 Cancel Booking

To cancel a booked seat,click on the My Booking link from the side menu to view the list of bookings. Then, click on the Cancel button against the booking to cancel it. Add Preferences

To add seat booking preferences,click on the My Preferences link from the side menu. Then, click on the Add New Preference button to select and save the preferences. Delete Preferences

To remove seat booking preferences,click on the My Preferences link from the side menu to view the list of selected preferences. Then, click on the Cancel button against the preference to remove it. View All Bookings

To view bookings across all the users, click on All Booking link from the side menu. Need more Help?
